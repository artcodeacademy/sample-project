Sample Project

This is a small sample of our tutorials, in each step you will be guided to find the best way to complete a project but it is really important to think outside the box and be creative!

Click here

1) Create a new project, choose a backdrop and an object.

2) When you press the green flag make the object start in the position you prefer.

3) Now make your character walk to a different position.

Hint: Look through the motion blocks, there are many ways to do this.

4) Make your character say or think something, for example: "I want to learn how to code."

Bonus: Try making your character turn or add a sound. Did you like it? Remember this is only a small sample, with our tutorials you'll learn to code your own games and much more.
